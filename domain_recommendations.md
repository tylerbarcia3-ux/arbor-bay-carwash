# Domain Recommendations - Arbor Bay CarWash

Choosing the right domain is crucial for local SEO and brand recognition. Here are my top recommendations:

## Recommended Domains
1. **arborbaycarwash.com** (Primary choice - exact name match)
2. **arborbaywash.com** (Short, catchy, easy to remember)
3. **arborbaycarwashpa.com** (Good if .com is taken, adds local relevance)

## Registration Tips
- **Register for 2+ years**: Shows search engines that you are established.
- **Enable Privacy Protection**: Keeps your personal/business contact info off the public WHOIS registry.
- **Short & Simple**: Avoid hyphens or complex spellings.
